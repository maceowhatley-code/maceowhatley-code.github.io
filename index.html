Thesis
Thesis

Bitcoin promised peer-to-peer cash. Ethereum promised a world computer. People around the world are using both to build genuine ownership of their economic lives. This show is about what that actually looks like.

State it directly. Advocate for it. White papers were modest — the human need is enormous — builders are the proof.

How It Flows

Podcast guest questions drive everything downstream.

01
Podcast Guest
Guest appears on Let's Talk About ETH and asks a question or raises a topic on air
02
WTM Bounty (Kenny)
That question becomes the week's WTM bounty prompt — posted by Kenny's team every week
03
Community Contest (Bayo)
Bayo's team runs 1 contest per week — community goes out, shoots content answering the prompt
04
Content Published
Submissions come in, Brian's graphics go up, content lives on the WTM channel and Farcaster
